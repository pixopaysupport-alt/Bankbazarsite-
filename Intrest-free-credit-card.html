Interest Free Period In Credit Card Kya Hota Hai?

Interest free period ek aisa time hota hai jisme aap credit card se purchase kar sakte ho aur agar due date se pehle full payment kar dete ho toh aapko koi interest nahi dena padta.

Simple words me bole toh bank aapko kuch din ke liye free me paise use karne deta hai.

Interest Free Period Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Har credit card ka ek billing cycle hota hai. Billing cycle complete hone ke baad bank bill generate karta hai aur payment due date deta hai.

  • Purchase Date
  • Billing Date
  • Due Date
  • Interest Calculation

Agar aap due date ke pehle pura payment kar dete ho toh interest charge nahi lagta.

Kab Interest Lagta Hai?

Agar aap due date tak full bill payment nahi karte ho toh bank interest charge karta hai.

Frequently Asked Questions

Interest Free Period Kitna Hota Hai?

Mostly banks 45 se 55 days tak interest free period dete hai.

Kya Minimum Due Pay Karne Par Interest Lagta Hai?

Haan. Minimum due pay karne par remaining amount par interest lag sakta hai.

Kya Cash Withdrawal Par Interest Free Period Milta Hai?

Nahi. Cash withdrawal par mostly instantly interest lagta hai.

Best Time Credit Card Use Karne Ka Kaunsa Hai?

Billing date ke just baad purchase karna best mana jata hai kyuki isse maximum interest free days milte hai.
