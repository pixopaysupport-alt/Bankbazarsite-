CVV Number Kya Hota Hai?

CVV ka full form Card Verification Value hota hai. Ye ek secret security code hota hai jo credit card aur debit card par diya hota hai.

CVV number mainly online payment verification ke liye use hota hai. Jab aap online payment karte ho tab CVV verify karta hai ki payment karne wale person ke paas asli card available hai.

CVV number hackers aur fraud transactions ko rokne me help karta hai. Isi wajah se almost har payment gateway CVV verification use karta hai.

CVV Number Card Me Kaha Hota Hai?

Mostly debit card aur credit card me CVV card ke piche printed hota hai. Ye generally 3 digit ka hota hai.

  • Visa Card → 3 Digit CVV
  • Mastercard → 3 Digit CVV
  • RuPay Card → 3 Digit CVV
  • American Express → 4 Digit CVV
CVV ko kabhi bhi social media ya unknown person ke sath share nahi karna chahiye.

CVV Number Ka Use Kya Hai?

CVV number mainly online payment aur online shopping me use hota hai. Ye extra security layer provide karta hai.

  • Amazon Shopping
  • Flipkart Payment
  • Mobile Recharge
  • Electricity Bill Payment
  • Netflix Subscription
  • UPI Linked Card Payment
  • Online Ticket Booking

Agar kisi person ke paas sirf aapka card number ho aur CVV na ho toh wo normally online transaction complete nahi kar payega.

CVV Number Online Payment Me Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Jab aap kisi website par payment karte ho tab aapse ye details mangi jati hai:

  • Card Holder Name
  • Card Number
  • Expiry Date
  • CVV Number

Uske baad bank aapke card details verify karta hai aur payment process hoti hai. CVV number verify karta hai ki card physically aapke paas hai.

Kya CVV Share Karna Safe Hai?

Nahi. Kisi bhi unknown person ke sath CVV share karna dangerous ho sakta hai.

Bank kabhi bhi phone call, SMS ya WhatsApp par CVV number nahi mangta.

Agar koi person aapse CVV maang raha hai toh wo fraud ho sakta hai.

CVV Number Secure Kaise Rakhe?

  • Kisi ke sath CVV share na kare
  • Unknown websites par payment avoid kare
  • OTP aur CVV dono secret rakhe
  • Public WiFi me online banking avoid kare
  • Browser me card save na kare
  • Fake apps se bachkar rahe
  • Regular bank alerts check kare
